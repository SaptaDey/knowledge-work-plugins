## Content Creation

### Blog Posts

```markdown
# [Headline]

**Meta Description:** [155 characters]
**Target Keyword:** [Keyword]

[Opening hook — statistic, question, or bold claim]

## [Section 1]
[Content with subheadings every 300 words]

## [Section 2]
[Short paragraphs, 2-4 sentences]

## Key Takeaways
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]

## [CTA Section]
[Clear call to action]
```

**Best Practices:**
- 8th-grade reading level
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Subheadings every 300 words
- Bullet points for scannability
- One CTA per post

### Email Marketing

```markdown
**Subject:** [50 characters max, action-oriented]
**Preview:** [40 characters]

[Personalized opening]

[Value proposition — benefits, not features]

[Social proof or data point]

[Clear, single CTA button]

[P.S. for secondary message]
```

**Subject Line Formulas:**
- How to [achieve outcome]
- [Number] ways to [solve problem]
- The [adjective] guide to [topic]
- [Name], [personalized hook]

### Social Media

**LinkedIn:**
- Hook in first line (before "see more")
- Professional but conversational
- Use line breaks for readability
- End with question or CTA

**Twitter/X:**
- Front-load the value
- Use threads for long-form
- Include visual when possible

**Instagram:**
- Lead with the caption hook
- Use emojis strategically
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags

### Landing Pages

```markdown
# [Headline — benefit-focused]

## [Subheadline — clarifies the offer]

[Hero section with visual]

## Value Propositions
- [Benefit 1]
- [Benefit 2]
- [Benefit 3]

## Social Proof
[Testimonial or logo bar]

## Objection Handling
[Address top 2-3 concerns]

## CTA
[Clear, action-oriented button]
```

## Campaign Planning

### OAMCM Framework

**Objective**: What success looks like (SMART format)
**Audience**: Who we're targeting (segments, personas)
**Message**: Core value proposition and proof points
**Channel**: Where we'll reach them
**Measure**: How we'll track success

### Campaign Brief Template

```markdown
# Campaign: [Name]

## Objective
[SMART goal]

## Target Audience
| Segment | Size | Characteristics |
|---------|------|-----------------|

## Key Message
**Primary**: [One sentence value prop]
**Proof Points**: [Supporting evidence]

## Channel Mix
| Channel | Role | Budget % |
|---------|------|----------|
| [Channel] | [Awareness/Consideration/Conversion] | [%] |

## Content Calendar
| Date | Channel | Asset | Owner |
|------|---------|-------|-------|

## Success Metrics
| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|

## Budget
| Category | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Total | $X |
```

### Channel Selection

**Owned:**
- Blog, Email, Social, Webinars, Podcasts

**Earned:**
- PR, Guest content, Influencer, Community, Reviews

**Paid:**
- Search ads, Social ads, Display, Sponsored content, Events

## Brand Voice

### Voice Attributes

Define on spectrums:
- **Formality**: Casual ←→ Formal
- **Authority**: Peer ←→ Expert
- **Emotion**: Reserved ←→ Enthusiastic
- **Complexity**: Simple ←→ Technical
- **Energy**: Calm ←→ Energetic

### Tone by Channel

| Channel | Tone Adjustment |
|---------|-----------------|
| Blog | Educational, thorough |
| Social | Conversational, punchy |
| Email | Personal, direct |
| Sales | Confident, helpful |
| Support | Empathetic, clear |
| Press | Professional, factual |

### Style Rules

**Do:**
- Use active voice
- Write at 8th-grade level
- Use "you" and "we"
- Be specific and concrete

**Don't:**
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Use excessive exclamation marks
- Write walls of text

## Competitive Analysis

### Research Areas

- **Positioning**: How they describe themselves
- **Messaging**: Key claims and proof points
- **Content Strategy**: Topics, formats, frequency
- **SEO**: Keywords they rank for
- **Social Presence**: Engagement and tone
- **Pricing**: Model and positioning

### Output Format

```markdown
# Competitive Analysis: [Competitor]

## Positioning
**Their Statement**: [How they position]
**Target Audience**: [Who they target]

## Messaging Comparison
| Theme | Them | Us |
|-------|------|-----|

## Content Gap Analysis
| Topic | Them | Us | Opportunity |
|-------|------|-----|-------------|

## Social Presence
| Platform | Followers | Engagement | Tone |
|----------|-----------|------------|------|

## Opportunities
- [Gap we can exploit]

## Threats
- [Strength to counter]
```

## Performance Analytics

### Key Metrics by Channel

**Email:**
| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Open Rate | 20-25% |
| CTR | 2-5% |
| Unsubscribe | <0.5% |

**Social:**
| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| Engagement Rate | Content resonance |
| Share Rate | Viral potential |
| CTR | Conversion potential |

**Paid:**
| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| CTR | Ad relevance |
| CPC | Efficiency |
| ROAS | Profitability |

**SEO:**
| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|

| Backlinks | Domain strength |
